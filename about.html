About

About this project.

I am currently pursuing postgraduate research in International Relations at the University of Sheffield, focused on climate inequality and how communities are affected by it. My work examines the structural power dynamics in global climate governance, including how emissions responsibility, economic loss and policy influence are distributed unevenly between countries.

The Climate Bill was built to present that research area as an accessible, data led resource, using figures drawn from official and academic sources rather than commentary.

Every figure on this site comes from official or authoritative sources, including NASA, NOAA, the WMO, the IPCC, the IEA, IRENA, the World Bank, the IMF, the United Nations, and Our World in Data. Each statistic links to its original source.
