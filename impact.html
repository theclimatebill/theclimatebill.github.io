The bill
Who is actually paying

Agreed. Not paid.

After thirty years of argument, the world created a fund to compensate countries for climate damage they did not cause.

$822m
Pledged to the Loss and Damage Fund
Total pledges since COP27. Around $448 million has been disbursed. The United States withdrew its pledge in 2025.
Source: UNFCCC
$395bn
Estimated annual loss and damage
A 2024 study in Nature put annual climate loss and damage at around $395 billion. Total pledges to date amount to under a quarter of one percent of a single year's damage.
Source: Nature, 2024
Pledged versus needed, per year
$822m pledged in total, against $395bn of damage every year
